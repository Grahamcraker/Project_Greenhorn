Vector2 KnightBear_TD_Windows.Gameplay.GameObject.Direction
getset

The direction this GameObject will move on the screen.

The idea of a direction is a little difficult at first glance. The purpose of a 2D Vector is to add the current position to this value. The resulting coordinate will be the new position. Ex. If I have a direction of (1,0) and add it to my current position, (10,10) the new position will be (11,10), resulting in an increased position on the X-axis.

Vector2 KnightBear_TD_Windows.Gameplay.GameObject.Forward
getset

The direction of the "Forward" direction of this GameObject's texture.

This property determines what is the forward direction of a texture. i.e. If a texture of a nightmare is looking up by default, the Forward property would equal (0,-1), representing an up direction.
